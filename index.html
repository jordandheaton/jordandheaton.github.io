A little about me

I'm an aspiring Information Systems student at Brigham Young University, working toward the Marriott School of Business, with a habit of showing up fully wherever I land — whether that's a ballroom competition floor, a mission in Atlanta, or a Blender tutorial at age ten.

I've spent years training alongside coaches from Poland, Russia, Italy, and Canada, which taught me as much about discipline and cross-cultural communication as any classroom has. I love to travel, too — Egypt, Greece, Israel, France, Italy, England, and more.

I care about building systems — and relationships — that actually serve people, and I'm currently exploring that at the intersection of technology, business, and global markets.
